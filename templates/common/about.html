Discover the Story Behind PAVAJ

From humble beginnings in Plovdiv’s creative district, Pavaj was born out of a love for honest food, seasonal ingredients, and soulful hospitality. Today, it continues to grow — rooted in tradition, shaped by vision, and inspired by the people it serves.
"Pavaj is not just a restaurant. It's a feeling — crafted through taste, people, and place."

Our Story

Where It All Began

Pavaj was born in 2015 in the heart of Kapana — Plovdiv’s vibrant creative district — with a simple idea: to bring people together around honest food made from the freshest local ingredients. What started as a small, cozy eatery quickly grew into one of Bulgaria’s most beloved restaurants, known for its seasonal menus, soul-filled atmosphere, and personal touch in every plate.

Each dish tells a story — of tradition, place, and the people behind it. Many of the vegetables we serve come from our own garden in the nearby village of Izbeglii, grown with care and respect for the land.

Our Mission

Food with Purpose

At Pavaj, our mission is to offer more than just a meal — we create meaningful culinary experiences rooted in seasonality, sustainability, and hospitality. We are committed to working with small local producers and farmers, sourcing only the highest quality ingredients, and treating each guest with warmth and authenticity.

We aim to nourish not only the body but also the senses and spirit — through taste, setting, and the care behind everything we do.

Our Vision

A Future Rooted in Seasonality and Soul

Our vision is to remain a beacon of authentic Balkan dining — a place where tradition meets creativity, and every visit feels both familiar and new. We aspire to grow sustainably while preserving our personal, community-based approach.

Whether it’s through our evolving seasonal menus, our own-grown produce, or our selection of Bulgarian wines and rakias, we want Pavaj to be remembered not only for its food — but for the feeling it leaves behind.

Our Values

At Pavaj, values aren’t just words — they’re lived in every decision we make, every ingredient we source, and every plate we serve. Meet the Founders

The People Behind the Vision

Pavaj was founded by Madlen and Raycho — two passionate souls united by their love for food, people, and meaningful hospitality. What began as a shared dream became a living, breathing space where flavors, creativity, and human connection meet every day.

With deep respect for local traditions and ingredients, they envisioned a restaurant that feels like home — where every guest is treated as a friend and every dish carries a piece of the land, the season, and the heart that created it.

Тogether, Madlen and Raycho built more than a restaurant.  They built Pavaj — a space full of taste, warmth, and story.
